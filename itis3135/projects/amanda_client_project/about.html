The Journey So Far

Playing right when Overwatch first released, Charlotte began her climb as a Tracer player of all things. That...didn't work out so after only a single season, she tried out mercy and found her love for the hero.

The grind was long, but in season 22 of Overwatch, she hit Grandmaster for the first time. Just a season later, she hit top 500 and has been playing in the same competitive scene since.

Behind the Stream

Streaming is a new experience for Charlotte, but she's started in an effort to build with a community and flex her Mercy muscles. She also wants to change the Mercy player scene and get them to use damage boost and chain boost instead of healbotting
